Who am I?

Hello to everyone reading! I am Alexis Cooper. I am a young black female. I was born in March of 2003. I am 19 years old.

Professional Life:

I am sophomore student at Elon University, listed on the Spring '22 President's List. I hope to have an internship within the next year.

I've worked since I was 15 years old. My first job was at a restaurant named 'Zesto's' which sold burgers, hotdogs, and icecream. I worked there for two years until it got shut down in 2020. I then worked at Krispy Kreme for a year until I graduated High School. I was seeking for higher pay, so that summer I quit Krispy Kreme and worked at Lowe's Home Improvement for 3 months. My overall job of as senator is to help facilitate student/faculty relations, to ensure that student concerns and needs are resolved, and to oversee student clubs and activities.
